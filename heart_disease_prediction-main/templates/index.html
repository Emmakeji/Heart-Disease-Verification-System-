Heart Disease Prediction System

This heart diesase prediction system is developed using XGBoost. The system uses 6 major medical parameters such as Chest pain type, Thalach (max heart rate achieved), Slope (slope of the peak exercise segment), RestECG, age, fasting blood sugar for prediction. The EHDPS predicts the likelihood of patients getting heart disease.
